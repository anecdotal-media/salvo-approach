02
How We Work
Our Approach to Social
for Salvo.
Every step grounded in Salvo’s mission and the communities they want to reach. Not a template — a system built for this org.
01
Deep Discovery & Audit
Comprehensive platform audit — performance data, audience, engagement benchmarks, competitive landscape. Paired with discovery sessions to understand Salvo’s objectives, voice, and what real success looks like beyond the metrics.
02
Social Objectives Tied to Brand Objectives
Every KPI we set maps directly to what Salvo is actually trying to accomplish — registrations, community visibility, brand recognition in underserved markets. We don’t chase vanity metrics. We chase outcomes.
03
Organic-First Growth Strategy
Paid reach without organic trust is a leaky bucket. We build the organic foundation first. Once that engine is running, paid amplifies what’s already working. This is how you build something that outlasts the ad spend.
04
Community & Audience Social Listening
We monitor the communities Salvo wants to reach — the soccer parent groups, youth athlete spaces, cultural communities, local networks. Their interests, questions, and language inform every content decision.
05
Prioritize Shareability & FYP Reach
Follower count is a lagging indicator. The real goal is organic reach — content that gets shared and lands on For You Pages. We engineer for that from the first frame. Hook, format, pacing, audio — all of it built to travel.
06
Hook First. Program Second.
Nobody watches content to be sold to. We lead with the story, the skill, the laugh, the moment — and bring Salvo in naturally. That sequencing is the difference between content that gets skipped and content that gets saved.
07
Playbook & Content Calendar
A defined content calendar with approval and editing workflows. A living playbook that governs brand voice, visual standards, platform best practices, and UGC. Built to scale — and to hand off. Salvo owns it permanently.
08
Quality Over Quantity
You don’t have to post every day to grow. You have to post the right things. We publish fewer pieces that earn real attention rather than volume that disappears. Every post earns its place on the calendar.
09
Sustainable Content Capture
Professional production visits anchor the library. Between those visits, Salvo’s coaches, staff, and volunteers become a guided capture team — equipped with their own playbook and virtual training to keep the content pipeline full.

04
Content Framework
Six Content Categories.
Every piece of content lives in one of these six buckets. Each serves a different audience need, platform behavior, and funnel stage — from awareness through conversion.
Category 01
Education
Content that teaches. Builds authority, earns trust, and gives people a reason to follow before they’re ready to register.
  • Coach to camera — drills for specific age groups
  • Soccer rules & ball sizes explained by age group
  • General youth soccer education (goal sizes, formats)
  • FAQ walkthroughs — financial aid, what to expect
  • Soccer fact videos leading to a Salvo CTA
  • Coach-to-parent tips and development philosophy
Category 02
Authenticity
Content that speaks to the real soccer community — the global game, the culture, the passion. Meeting people where they already live online.
  • Soccer history explained by the players themselves
  • Soccer terms explained on camera
  • All-time favorite goal and player interviews
  • Favorite player deep-dives by the kids
  • Soccer culture moments — boots, tactics, the game
  • Collaboration posts with other clubs or creators
Category 03
Storytelling
The content that moves people. Real stories from inside Salvo’s community — the ones that make a parent stop and think “that’s the club for my kid.”
  • Storytime interviews — how long they’ve played, why they love it
  • Player journeys and personal milestones
  • Family stories — how Salvo made it possible
  • Comedic documentary style — post-game interview parodies
  • “Coach said you showed up late… mind explaining that?”
  • Day-in-the-life content from coaches and players
Category 04
Highlight Reels
The content players want to share because it makes them look good. When a player sends Salvo’s reel to their friends, that’s free recruiting.
  • Game and training highlights — slow mo, music, cinematic
  • Dribble move tutorials by skilled players
  • Tactical highlights — tiki-taka, team play
  • Meta glasses POV dribbling footage
  • Nice touches set to classical music
  • Trying to replicate famous goals
Category 05
Viral Trends
Fast-moving, shareable content built for discovery. The formats that land on For You Pages and reach people who’ve never heard of Salvo.
  • Corner goal challenge
  • 1-touch challenge
  • Ball trap challenge
  • Trending audio and format adaptations
  • Competitions between players — filmed for social
  • Replicate famous goals — challenge format
Category 06
UGC
Content created by the Salvo community itself — parents, players, coaches, and volunteers. The most authentic content we can publish because it’s real, and everyone knows it.
  • Parent sideline captures and celebrations
  • Player self-filmed skills and moments
  • Volunteer and staff perspective content
  • Community reactions and event coverage
  • Defined UGC accounts sourced during discovery
  • Prompted submissions via the capture playbook

05
Execution
Content Capture Plan
A great strategy is worthless without the footage to bring it to life. Here’s how we build a sustainable content supply chain that doesn’t burn anyone out.
🎥
On-Site Production
Dedicated production visits — game days, training sessions, camps, community events. All travel and accommodations included. The raw material that fuels months of content across every platform. This is what makes the videos actually work — you can’t manufacture this footage remotely.
📱
Staff & Volunteer Capture Army
Coaches, staff, players, and volunteers are on the ground every day. Equipped with a dedicated capture playbook, shot lists, and best-practices training, they become a distributed content team — supplying the raw, in-the-moment footage that no crew can plan for.
📅
Defined Content Calendar
Every post planned by platform, format, and objective. Nothing goes live without a purpose. Built around Salvo’s season, registration windows, events, and key cultural moments in the soccer calendar. No guesswork, no scrambling.
✅
Approval & Editing Process
A clear workflow from raw capture to published post. Salvo has final eyes on everything before it goes live. Consistent quality, consistent brand, no surprises. Every edit, graphic, and caption reviewed against the playbook.
🎓
Virtual Training for Best Practices
Best practices training for Salvo’s internal capture team. How to film, what to look for, how to submit. Simple enough that a volunteer can do it well — good enough that we can actually use the footage to build professional content.
🧳
Defined UGC Pipeline
Identified community accounts, prompts, permissions, and a submission process established during discovery. We define what UGC looks like for Salvo, activate the right contributors, and build a system that generates authentic content from within the community itself.

What Success Looks Like
Expected Outcomes.
The compounding results of doing this right and giving it the runway it needs. Not promises tied to a sprint — momentum built over time.
↑
Increased Awareness
Families who’ve never heard of Salvo will — across cultural backgrounds, income levels, and soccer journeys. The club becomes a recognized name in communities it has never meaningfully reached before.
↑
Community Buzz
Salvo becomes a talking point — in parent groups, at fields, in online soccer communities. The kind of organic conversation that no ad budget can manufacture on its own.
↑
Earned Media Mentions
Content interesting enough to be shared or referenced by other accounts, creators, and local media — without paid placement.
↑
Vocal Advocates — Word of Mouth
Families who found Salvo through social become the club’s most powerful recruiting tool. A parent sharing a video with their network is worth more than any targeted ad.
↑
Increased Interest & Inquiries
More families in the funnel. More DMs, form fills, and registration questions. The pipeline grows as awareness and trust compound over time.
↑
Increased Involvement & Sign-Ups
The bottom line. Every part of this strategy exists to move families from discovery to registration. Organic builds the trust. Paid closes the gap.
→
Opportunity to Expand
Because awareness grows outside of targeted areas, Salvo’s reach begins to extend beyond the southeast metro — opening doors to new partnerships, new markets, stronger donor relationships, and a brand presence that reflects the true quality of this organization.
